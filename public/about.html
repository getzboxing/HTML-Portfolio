About Me

Coach and former athletic competitor, I am committed to finding news way to interact with technology to improve and expand atletic performance.

My concentration has been with combat sports, with a heavy concentration in boxing. I use the borrowed phrase to describe my goals: 'Improving the Sweet Science by eliminating the brutal physics'

I will offer what research has revealed about the impact combat sports have on society, and vice versa.

We will have a look at the moral principles that relate to boxing, and other combat sports, particularly as how they contribute to concussive injury and how that may be prevented.
